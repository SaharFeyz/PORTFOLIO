—Arc'teryx Veilance

This group project was done in an upper level UX Design course. The core concept of the project was rooted in Experience Design.

...

It employs design-driven, design thinking, effective use of technology as a media, that addresses a core business problem to find an opportunity to intervene, and importantly is after unique and memorable outcomes. For this project, our team chose Arc'teryx, specifically Veilance, to improve the Veilance visibility and importance of its innovative fabrications to the potential consumers in its website. The interesting outcome of this project, which was done in Fall 2017, is that after our team proposed the final digital prototype, the similar main changes that we proposed, has been taken place in Arc'teryx actual website in 2020. In its main landing page, Arc'teryx has added a link to Veilance webpage, and an Editorial section has been separately added to emphasize more intriguing information about the product for more customer engagement.
