ETHICS & AESTHETICS

Project Overview

DESCRIPTION

Ethics & Aesthetics is the first fashion and sustainable living journal targeted to professionals looking to make waves in the industry. It is a lifestyle publication for those who are creative, curious and care about nature, that aims to inspire a connection with nature, considered ways of living, environmental stewardship and a sense of fulfillment in our everyday lives.

MY MAJOR ROLE

  • Print and Web Component Design
  • Publishing and P&L
  • SWOT Analysis

CHALLENGES

  • Designing components that align with the mission of the brand
  • Calculation of P&L sheet, forecasting cash inflow (revenue) and cash outflow (expenses)
