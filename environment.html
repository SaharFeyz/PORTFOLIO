3D VIRTUAL ENVIRONMENT

Project Overview

DESCRIPTION

The atmosphere I aimed to create demonstrates a photorealistic kitchen and a middle countertop that displays a simple vase yet luxury looking through the use of lighting and Phong material. The textures used for cabinets are Lambert shaded and wooden textures to absorb the light in contrary to the vase that reflects multiple lights. There is directional lighting outside of the window to create the shadows, and multiple area lights to brighten the environment. The darker image uses less lights to demonstrate that some internal lights in the kitchen are turned off.

MY MAJOR ROLE

  • Modeling the kitchen, Lighting the environment and adding textures
