Process

About the company

The company (Charcoal) is the supplier of arts and crafts material. It also offers occasional classes/clubs on how to make different crafts, arts, and decorations. The company also provides places and supplies for birthday parties.

Potential customers

  • parents: The parents are one group of stakeholders that are the potential customer of the company, since they need to provide art/craft supplies for their kids. They also need to plan a birthday party for their children, so the company is a perfect supplier of craft and birthday events for these customers.
  • students: Some courses at school such as art, requires art supplies and some basic skills in painting and sketching. The company can provide these needs for students by holding classes/events and tutorials.
  • novice/enthusiast crafters: These customers are potentially in the range of 15-25 years old and they are interested in making art and craft in their spare time, but they need inspiration and guidance. The company provides these customers a platform to help them start and finish a project and inspire them to make creative works.
  • kids: The kids enjoy drawing with crayons and creating crafts with their peers, so the company is a good place to gather these kids together ,create fun for them and supply them with tools.

5 tasks that users would accomplish

  • Clients can browse through different crafts material categories and find out about the detailed information such as price, size, and material.
  • Clients can check out the time and location of the seasonal/weekly events/classes that company provides in their brick and mortar stores.
  • Clients can make a reservation online for a spot to hold a birthday party.
  • Clients can place an order of suggested pre packaged craft materials to accomplish a specific project e.g. pre packaged beads and chains to make necklace and bracelet.
  • Clients can create an account to receive coupons and promotions.

Objective

  • Defining the basic branding ideas
  • Listing the tasks that users would come to the site to accomplish
  • Defining the possible users

Final Reflection

Analysis

The overall functionality and sketch process of the project was done successfully, and the project met the expectations. However, due to limited time we were not able to create a .html webpage for all the products, links, and buttons, in fact it was not required.

Takeaways

Sketching and wireframing was an essential part of this project, and it really helped to simplify the flow of the website in the coding phase.

Understanding the importance of ARIA role attributes in code for increasing the accessibility of the website, and creating a user-centered design.
