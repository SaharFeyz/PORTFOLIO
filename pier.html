PIER 1 IMPORTS

Project Overview

DESCRIPTION

The DESIGN ROOM page extension idea is a UX project that is focused on increasing the e-commerce sales of Pier 1 Imports. Its goal is to enhance the user experience while browsing and shopping for the furniture and home accessories through using moodboard as a filter.

MY MAJOR ROLES

  • Research the client and finding the business problem
  • Research for insights and Ideating possible digital intrusions that could give leverage in e-commerce sales
  • Customer journey framework
  • Preparing presentation slides
  • Graphs and their content (Ansoff Matrix brand model, customer journey framework, Persona, Project roadmap)

CHALLENGES

  • Finding an opportunity in the existing context and utilize that to improve the business of Pier 1 Imports brand
  • Adding value proposition to the brand by designing a new way of searching products

SOLUTIONS

  • Researching for insights and new target audience

DIGITAL TOOLS USED

  • Adobe Illustrator
  • InVision
  • Figma

Sprint Process

Target audience

  • Young first time home buyers who are looking for home furnishing products to decorate their first home.

Frame

  • How can Pier 1 Imports meet the needs of a new generation of audience while increasing their online sales?

Sprint questions

  • Can a new e-commerce strategy increase profit margin?
  • Does attracting new customers help in increasing Pier 1 imports sale and profit margin?

Sprint end goal

  • Increase the profit margin to payoff e-commerce expenses.

VALUE PROPOSITION

Customer

  • Simplifying the process of searching for furniture and lowering the cognitive overhead
  • Inspiring and guiding through search
  • Encourage creativity and designing

Business

  • Increasing e-commerce traffic and profit margin
  • Bringing in the value of “choose what speaks to you online”
  • Be a part of the customer's journey in designing

VAN UX CRITERIA

Elegance and Clarity

  • Consistency of visuals and elements with simple interactions, reduce the cognitive overhead
  • Easy and familiar paths for a straightforward navigation, lowers the friction for customers in searching for products

Impact

  • The audience will have an opportunity to engage more deeply in the website, that triggers them to purchase

Final Reflection

Analysis

According to the feedback from the teaching staff and guests in the room, our final presentation showed a lot of improvement from the beginning of the project, and the idea of moodboard was quite interesting. Also, our insight that Pier 1 Imports is falling behind from its competitors in commerce is intriguing, and there are opportunities to work on that.

Takeaways

The very valuable takeaway of this project is doing the initial research, understanding the business problem, the client company, the target audience, the insights, and performing an iterative process to achieve the best results.
