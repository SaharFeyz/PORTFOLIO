CHOPLOGIK ONLINE STORE

Design Evaluation

Project Overview

DESCRIPTION

The purpose of this study is to determine an optimal design for the efficiency (speed) of purchasing products, for both the product page(s) and shopping cart of the online store. It also aims to navigational issues and insights towards brand Perception. Choplogik is the brand name of Vancouver-based independent artist and sole proprietor Russell Alton. This study evaluates their online store ( choplogik.org ), where they sell prints and t-shirts. In this project, I used a controlled study method to test the website on 17 users.

MY MAJOR ROLE

  • Heuristic Evaluation
  • User testing (Controlled Study Method)
  • Writing and analysis

DIGITAL TOOLS USED

  • Figma

Process

Step 1:

  • Find an industry or community partner that we can work with, meet with them to explore their needs and what design we can evaluate.

Step 2:

  • Draft a study plan that documents how we could conduct a study that included all components, including the questionnaires, study tasks, interview questions (if needed), etc. We should also write one page that explains why we would not choose other methods to conduct the study.

Step 3:

  • Perform and conduct the study with an agreed upon number of participants (20 for our study).

Step 4:

  • Analyze our findings using known methods that are taught in class, and create a report that details our findings.
